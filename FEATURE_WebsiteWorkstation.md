# Feature Spec: Website Workstation (Quick Mockup Generator)

**Status:** Captured — build after first 5-10 real sales conversations
**Priority:** After outreach engine is actively being used

---

## Concept

A built-in tool in KOS that generates a basic website mockup for a lead so Jay has something visual to show during or after a sales call. Not a full website build — just enough to say "here's what yours could look like" and close the deal.

## What It Does

1. Takes lead data (business name, industry, services, phone, service area) + research findings
2. Generates brand identity if they don't have one:
   - Color palette (with reasoning — why these colors fit their industry/brand)
   - Font pairing
   - Logo placeholder/concept
   - Every decision explained with intent, not random
3. Produces a basic 1-page website mockup:
   - Hero section with headline + CTA
   - Services section
   - Reviews/social proof
   - Contact/booking section
   - Footer
4. Uses the lead's actual business info, real reviews, real services
5. Exportable as a screenshot or shareable link Jay can text/email to the prospect

## Design Decisions (Core Principle)

Every design choice must have reasoning:
- Color palette: based on industry psychology, competitor landscape, target customer
- Layout: based on what converts for home service websites specifically
- Copy: pulled from their actual reviews and services, not generic
- CTA: specific to their business ("Get a Free Estimate" not "Learn More")

## Tech Considerations

- Google Stitch for UI design generation — explore as potential tool
- Could also be a Next.js template system with dynamic data injection
- Templates by industry (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, etc.)
- Output: static HTML preview or screenshot

## Why Wait

Jay needs real sales conversations first to understand:
- What objections come up that a mockup would overcome
- What level of detail prospects need to see to say yes
- Whether the mockup is needed before or after the call
- What format works best (link, screenshot, PDF)

## Build After

- 5-10 real outreach conversations completed
- At least 1 website sold through the current manual process
- Clear understanding of where in the sales flow this tool fits
